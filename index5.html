To save you the trouble, I have gathered together some organizations and adoption agencies through the Sonoma Country foster system in California. Everything you need to know all in one place.

Agencies

Lilliput

Lilliput is a Foster Adoption Service located 2255 challenger way suit 100 Santa Rosa, CA 95407.  Phone: (707)603-3500 Email: santa.rosa.office@lilliput.org

Social Advocates for Youth (SAY)

SAY is an organization that provides support, oppertunities and hope to children, youth and families. They are located 2447 summerfield Rd, Santa Rosa, CA 95405. Phone: (707)544-3299

TLC Child and Family Services, Transitional Housing Program (THP)

TLC is an adoption agency and a housing program for young adults ages 16-24 who are in the foster system. TLC is located 821 Mendocino Ave, Santa Rosa, CA 95401. Phone: (707)528-3020 Email: info@tlc4kids.org

VOICES Youth Center

Voices is a Youth Center designed to help youth transition into adulthood. VOICES is located 714 Mendocino Ave, Santa Rosa, CA 95401. Phone: (707)579-4327

Alternative Family Services (AFS)

AFS is an adoption agency located 1421 Guerneville Rd, Santa Rosa, CA 95403 Phone: (707)576-7700 Email: info@afs4kids.org

Community Action Partnership

Community Action Partnership is an organization that reaches out to low income families, to help financially. CAP is located 2685 Irby St Florence, SC 29505. phone: (843)678-3400
